About the Project

This webshop is designed to provide a seamless online shopping experience for customers seeking high-quality yarn, knitting utensils, and interior products. With a user-friendly interface, the site features intuitive navigation, attractive product displays, and a responsive design that works well on both desktop and mobile devices. To enhance functionality, the webshop integrates Shopify's Customer Account API, allowing customers to create and manage their accounts easily, update their profiles, and view their order history. This feature ensures a personalized shopping experience, fostering customer loyalty. Additionally, the webshop utilizes the Payment Apps API to offer various secure payment options. This integration enables smooth transaction processing and customizable checkout experiences tailored to the needs of customers in Norway. The contact section includes essential information, such as the store address and a Google Map for easy location, fostering trust and accessibility. With a flexible search functionality, users can quickly find specific products, ensuring a smooth and efficient shopping experience. This project aims to create an engaging and efficient platform for knitting enthusiasts and home decor lovers alike.

Key Features

  • User-Friendly Interface: Intuitive design for easy navigation.
  • Product Showcase: Attractive product boxes with clear pricing.
  • Contact Section: Includes essential info and a Google Map.
  • Search Functionality: Quick search bar for easy product access.
